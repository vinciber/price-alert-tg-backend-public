Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Welcome to Price Alert. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our services:

  • User ID: A unique identifier to sync your alerts across devices.
  • Email: If provided, used for sending alerts and account recovery.
  • Telegram ID: If connected, used to send alerts via Telegram.
  • Device Data: Basic info for notification delivery and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Deliver price alerts and notifications.
  • Manage your subscription and supporter status.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use third-party services for specific functions:

  • Stripe: For payment processing.
  • Supabase: For secure data storage.
  • Firebase: For cloud messaging and analytics.

4. Your Rights

You can request data deletion or view your stored information by contacting support via the Feedback section in the app.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
